About LPRC OILERS FC

Our History

LPRC OILERS FC was founded in 1995 by a group of football enthusiasts in Monrovia, Liberia. What started as a local community team has grown into one of Liberia's most successful football clubs.

Over the years, we've remained committed to developing local talent and promoting the sport across Liberia. Our black and gold colors represent the strength and excellence we strive for both on and off the field.

1980

LPRC OILERS FC is founded in Monrovia by Samuel Johnson and a group of local football enthusiast

1991

The team wins its first Liberian Premier League title, establishing itself as a force in Liberian football.

2008

Oilers Arena becomes the official home stadium after extensive renovations funded by local businesses.

2015

LPRC OILERS FC launches its youth academy to develop the next generation of Liberian football talent.

2021

The team wins its 7th Liberian Premier League title, cementing its legacy as one of Liberia's most successful clubs.

Our Mission

At LPRC OILERS FC, our mission extends beyond winning matches. We are committed to:

  • Developing local football talent through our youth academy
  • Promoting sportsmanship and integrity in Liberian football
  • Engaging with our community through outreach programs
  • Representing Liberia with pride in international competitions

We believe that football has the power to unite communities and inspire the next generation of Liberians.
