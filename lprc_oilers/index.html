WELCOME TO LPRC OILERS FC

Liberia's Premier Football Team

Founded in the late 1980, LPRC OILERS FC has established itself as one of Liberia's most successful football clubs. With a rich history of championships and a commitment to developing local talent, we continue to represent excellence both on and off the field.

Our home stadium, Oilers Arena in Monrovia, hosts thousands of passionate fans each season who come to support our black and gold warriors.
